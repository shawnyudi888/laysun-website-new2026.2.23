Application System 04

Outdoor Engineered System

UV-stable and weather-resistant artificial greenery systems designed for exterior commercial applications, including streetscapes, outdoor malls, and building facades.

Outdoor Environment Visualization

Industry Challenge

  • Extreme UV exposure causing color fading
  • Temperature extremes (-20°C to +50°C)
  • Wind loads and structural stability
  • Rain, snow, and humidity degradation
  • Public safety and liability concerns

System Approach

  • UV-stabilized polymers rated for 5000+ hours exposure
  • Thermal expansion engineering for extreme climates
  • Wind-resistant anchoring systems (up to 150km/h)
  • Drainage-integrated modules preventing water retention
  • Anti-bird and pest-resistant design features
