Big-Box Retail Solutions

Optimized for High Turnover & Landed Cost

In the competitive world of Big-Box retail, an artificial plant is more than a product—it's a logistical headache and a high-stakes shelf-space investment. Laysun understands the pressures of retail buyers: from shipping efficiency to compliance risks.

  • Retail-Ready Packaging (RRP)

    PDQ display boxes and bespoke color box designs that cut in-store labor by up to 30% and maximize shelf visibility.

  • Logistical Optimization

    CBM reduction through optimized product structure and nesting designs, cutting landed costs by up to 15%.

  • Strict Compliance

    BSCI/SEDEX audited for ethical sourcing, ensuring your supply chain is bulletproof against regulatory risks.

Commercial Interior Solutions

Safety First: Inherently Fire Retardant Greenery

In modern commercial architecture—from luxury hotel lobbies to Grade-A office atriums—greenery is no longer a luxury; it is an essential element of biophilic design that enhances human well-being and property value.

  • Inherently Fire Retardant (IFR)

    Technology integrated into the material, not topical sprays. Meets NFPA 701 (US) and B1 (Europe) standards.

  • Zero Maintenance OPEX

    Eliminate ongoing costs of irrigation, pruning, and replacement. One-time investment with 10-year aesthetic lifespan.

  • Custom Spatial Branding

    Bespoke installations from 12ft statement trees to intricate green walls that align with your interior's DNA.

Outdoor Engineering Solutions

Engineered for the Elements

Outdoor landscapes are the first touchpoint of any premium property, yet they are constantly under siege by UV radiation, humidity, and temperature fluctuations. Traditional artificial plants fail within months under the sun.

  • Military-Grade UV Protection

    Proprietary UV-Stable technology tested for over 5000 hours of accelerated weathering. Guaranteed color retention.

  • Thermal & Wind Resilience

    Engineered to resist thermal expansion and withstand specific wind loads, ensuring public safety and durability.

  • Eco-Conscious Durability

    Extended replacement cycles reduce waste and achieve long-term sustainability goals in landscape investments.
