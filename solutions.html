Architectural Greenery for High-Traffic Commercial Interiors

Safety First: Inherently Fire Retardant Greenery (NFPA 701/B1)

In modern commercial architecture — from luxury hotel lobbies to Grade-A office atriums — greenery is no longer a luxury; it is an essential element of biophilic design. However, the challenges of live plants often hinder grand visions. Laysun provides the bridge between botanical aesthetics and commercial feasibility.

  • Safety First (Inherently Fire Retardant) NFPA 701 (US) and B1 (Europe) certified. IFR technology integrated into material for life-long protection
  • Operational Efficiency (Zero Maintenance) Eliminate irrigation, pruning, and replacement costs. 10-year aesthetic lifespan, maintenance-free
  • Custom Spatial Branding Bespoke installations from 12ft statement trees to intricate green walls, aligned with your interior's DNA
  • Hypoallergenic & Clean Ideal for healthcare and hospitality — eliminates soil-borne pests, pollen, and humidity issues

Engineered for the Elements: High-Performance Outdoor Landscapes

Engineered for the Elements: 5000h+ UV Stability

Outdoor landscapes are the first touchpoint of any premium property, yet they are constantly under siege by UV radiation, humidity, and temperature fluctuations.   • Military-Grade UV Protection Proprietary UV-Stable technology tested for 5000+ hours. Guaranteed color retention even in high-UV regions
  • Thermal Resilience Engineered to resist thermal expansion from desert heat to sub-zero winters, preventing cracking or brittleness
