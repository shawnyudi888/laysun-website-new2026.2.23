Fire Safety Engineering

All fire-rated systems undergo rigorous third-party testing to ensure compliance with international building codes. Test reports and classification documents provided for regulatory submission.

Reaction to Fire Testing

EN 13501-1 evaluates ignitability, flame spread, heat release, smoke production, and burning droplets. LAYSUN systems achieve Class B-s1,d0 rating.

Surface Burning Characteristics

ASTM E84 (Steiner Tunnel Test) measures flame spread index and smoke developed index. Systems rated Class A (FSI 0-25, SDI 0-450).

Textile Fire Tests

NFPA 701 evaluates flame propagation of textiles and films. Method 1 for fabrics, Method 2 for vinyl-coated fabrics and barriers.
