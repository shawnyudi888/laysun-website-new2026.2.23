Our Customization Process

From initial concept to final installation, we guide you every step of the way

STEP 01

Discovery

Initial consultation to understand your vision, space requirements, and aesthetic goals. We review floor plans, mood boards, and reference images.

Duration: 1-2 days
STEP 02

Design & Sampling

Our designers create detailed 3D renderings and physical samples. We refine specifications until every detail meets your expectations.

Duration: 1-2 weeks
STEP 03

Production

Precision manufacturing with rigorous quality control. Regular progress updates and photos from the factory floor.

Duration: 2-6 weeks
STEP 04

Delivery & Install

White-glove delivery and professional installation. Final walkthrough to ensure everything exceeds your expectations.

Duration: 1-2 weeks
