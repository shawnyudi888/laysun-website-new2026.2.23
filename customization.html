Custom Service Process

From initial concept to final installation, our streamlined process ensures Architectural-Grade results at every stage.

01

Discovery & Consultation

Day 1-2

We begin with understanding your vision, spatial requirements, and brand identity. Our design team conducts site analysis and mood board development.

02

AI-Enhanced Concept Design

Day 3-5

Our AI engine generates multiple design iterations. You receive photorealistic 3D renders and material samples for review.

03

48H Rapid Prototyping

Day 6-7

Physical prototype creation using advanced 3D printing and hand-finishing techniques. Review and refine before full production.

04

Engineering & Compliance

Day 8-14

Structural engineering for safety, fire retardancy integration (NFPA 701/B1), and UV stabilization testing for outdoor applications.

05

Production & Quality Control

Day 15-30

Full-scale manufacturing with batch testing. Each piece undergoes rigorous quality checks against Architectural-Grade standards.

06

Global Delivery & Installation

Day 31-45

Optimized logistics for safe transport. Optional installation support and final on-site adjustments for perfect presentation.
