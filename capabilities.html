Sustainability & Environmental Responsibility

At Laysun, we believe that exceptional design should not come at the expense of our planet. Our commitment to sustainability is woven into every aspect of our operations.

By significantly extending the replacement cycle through superior engineering, we help project owners reduce waste and achieve long-term sustainability goals in their landscape investments.

  • Eco-friendly PE materials and recyclable packaging
  • Water-based adhesives and low-VOC production processes
  • Extended product lifecycle reducing replacement waste
  • Carbon footprint optimization in logistics
  • Continuous R&D in biodegradable materials
