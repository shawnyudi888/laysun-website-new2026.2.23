ENGINEERED FOR EXCELLENCE

From our manufacturing facilities in China to our global headquarters in the United States, we are redefining commercial botanical solutions through precision engineering and sustainable practices.

OUR PURPOSE

Global Leadership in
Artificial Horticulture

Our Mission: Laysun致力于为全球商业空间提供高质量、可持续的仿真园艺解决方案。我们结合创新技术与精湛设计，打造出既美观又环保的园艺系统，帮助企业提升空间美感，减少维护成本，并符合严格的消防安全标准。

Our Vision: 成为全球领先的仿真园艺系统服务商，打造绿色、低碳、智能的商业景观，让每一处空间都充满自然的生命力。

Founded on the principles of engineering precision and botanical artistry, Laysun bridges the gap between nature's beauty and commercial practicality. We don't just create artificial plants; we engineer sustainable ecosystems for the built environment.
