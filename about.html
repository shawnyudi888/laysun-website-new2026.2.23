Global Artificial Horticulture Systems Platform

Engineering-first approach to commercial green infrastructure. Structured for safety, scalability, and global compliance.

Engineering Infrastructure, Not Selling Products

LAYSUN was established with a fundamental rethinking of how artificial horticulture integrates into commercial environments. We do not manufacture decorative items. We engineer structural systems that meet building codes, support life safety requirements, and enable scalable global deployment.

"Our mission is to transform commercial spaces through engineered botanical systems that combine structural integrity, regulatory compliance, and long-term durability."

Every system component is developed through rigorous engineering analysis, third-party testing, and real-world deployment validation. This approach ensures that LAYSUN systems perform not just on day one, but across decades of commercial operation.

Development Timeline

Key milestones in our evolution from component supplier to global systems platform.

2006

Foundation

Established as engineering consultancy for architectural greening systems. Focus on structural analysis and compliance consulting.

2010

System Development

Launched first modular structural system with integrated fire safety. Achieved initial EN 13501-1 certification.

2014

Global Expansion

Opened regional offices in Europe and Middle East. Established global supply chain for multi-country project execution.

2018

Platform Integration

Integrated CRM, project management, and compliance documentation systems. Achieved ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certification.

2022

Systems Platform Launch

Repositioned as Global Artificial Horticulture Systems Platform. Launched four application-specific system architectures.

2024

Digital Transformation

Deployed BIM-integrated design tools, automated compliance documentation, and real-time project tracking systems.
