Our Story

For over 20 years, Laysun has been more than just a manufacturer — we are a global solution provider where botanical art meets industrial engineering.

Born from a vision to bring nature's soul into the built environment, we have evolved into a dual-engine powerhouse: a high-end Design Agency and a large-scale Manufacturing Hub.

While others simply copy nature, we reimagine it. Our in-house design team leverages AI-driven trend forecasting and 3D modeling to turn global aesthetic shifts into market-ready collections.

Operating out of our 10,000㎡ precision facility, we harmonize traditional craftsmanship with modern automation. Every leaf texture and trunk silhouette is an Architectural-Grade masterpiece.
