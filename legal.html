Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

LAYSUN Global Systems ("LAYSUN", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our commercial services.

This policy applies to all personal data processed by LAYSUN in the course of our business operations, including data collected through our website, project inquiries, and commercial relationships.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Submit project inquiries or requests for proposals
  • Register for documentation downloads
  • Subscribe to technical updates or newsletters
  • Contact our engineering or sales teams
  • Apply for employment opportunities

This information may include:

  • Contact Information: Name, business email, phone number, company name, job title
  • Business Information: Company address, industry, company size
  • Project Information: Project location, requirements, timeline, budget range
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage patterns

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • IP address and geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website addresses

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Responding to inquiries, preparing project proposals, and delivering requested documentation
  • Business Operations: Processing transactions, managing accounts, and maintaining commercial relationships
  • Communication: Sending technical updates, project information, and service notifications
  • Improvement: Analyzing website usage to improve user experience and service offerings
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and protecting our legal rights

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations or respond to your requests
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests, provided your rights do not override these interests
  • Consent: Processing based on your explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time
  • Legal Obligation: Processing necessary to comply with applicable laws and regulations

5. Your Data Protection Rights

📋Right to Access

You have the right to request copies of your personal data and information about how we process it.

✏️Right to Rectification

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

🗑️Right to Erasure

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.

🚫Right to Restrict

You have the right to request restriction of processing your personal data.

📤Right to Portability

You have the right to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

🛑Right to Object

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your personal data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, CRM systems, email delivery, and analytics providers
  • Professional Advisors: Legal, accounting, and insurance professionals
  • Regulatory Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal data and to treat it in accordance with the law.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Inquiry Data: 3-5 years for commercial relationship management
  • Project Data: Duration of project plus 7 years for legal compliance
  • Marketing Data: Until consent is withdrawn or 2 years of inactivity
  • Analytics Data: 14 months (anonymized after this period)

8. International Data Transfers

As a global organization, we may transfer your data to countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). When we do, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions for countries recognized by the EU
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-group transfers

Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How We Use Cookies

LAYSUN uses cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for website functionality, security, and user authentication. Cannot be disabled. Session - 1 year
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous usage data. 14 months
Marketing Used to track visitors across websites to enable relevant and engaging marketing content. 6 months

Specific Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

  • laysun_session: Maintains your session state across page requests
  • laysun_consent: Stores your cookie consent preferences
  • csrf_token: Security token for form submission protection

Analytics Cookies

  • _ga (Google Analytics): Distinguishes unique users
  • _gid (Google Analytics): Distinguishes users for 24 hours
  • _gat (Google Analytics): Throttles request rate

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

Most web browsers allow some control of cookies through browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner requesting your consent for non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies (only essential cookies will be used)
  • Customize your preferences for specific cookie types

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website.
