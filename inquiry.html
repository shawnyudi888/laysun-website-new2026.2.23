Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our inquiry and project process.

How quickly will I receive a response?
+

A-level inquiries receive response within 24 business hours. B-level within 48 hours. All inquiries receive automatic confirmation email with inquiry reference number immediately upon submission.

What information do I need to provide for an accurate proposal?
+

Project location, estimated area, application type (hospitality/retail/etc.), and required compliance standards are essential. Architectural drawings or photos are helpful but not required for initial inquiry.

Do you provide samples before full project commitment?
+

Yes, sample programs are available for qualified commercial projects. Sample requests are processed as C-level inquiries and include material swatches, fire test samples, and structural mock-ups where applicable.

Can you support multi-country rollouts?
+

Absolutely. Our global manufacturing and logistics network supports coordinated multi-country deployments with consistent quality and localized compliance documentation per jurisdiction.

Is my project information confidential?
+

Yes. All project information is handled under strict confidentiality. NDA agreements are available upon request and can be executed prior to detailed technical discussions.
